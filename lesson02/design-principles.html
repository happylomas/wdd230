PARC: Repetition

Amazon

amazon.com

Repetition helps to create a sense of unity and to reinforce the overall message. That way, item are placed in a logical and cohesive manner that helps the user to know the concept of the element. Looking at the screenshot from Amazon page you will that the size of all the items are the same, the front are also the same This make the user to know where to fix their eyes or visual, if they want to look very well at the item they want to buy or if they want to know the name of the product.
